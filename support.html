How do I play?

Every puzzle is a phrase where each letter is hidden behind a number. Tap a number, then tap the letter you think it is — every tile with that number fills in at once. Solve the whole phrase to win.

I'm stuck on a puzzle.

Use a power-up — a Hammer reveals one letter, a Rocket reveals a whole word, and a Shield absorbs a wrong guess. You can also grab a free hint on Easy and Medium levels.

Is my progress saved?

Yes — everything is saved on your device automatically. Deleting the app will erase your progress.

Does the game work offline?

Yes, completely. Word Hoot needs no internet connection.

Do you collect my data?

No. See our Privacy Policy — the app collects nothing.
